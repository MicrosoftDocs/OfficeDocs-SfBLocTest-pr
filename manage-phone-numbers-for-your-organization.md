# Manage phone numbers for your organization

Each country or region has different instructions for getting phone numbers for Skype for Business Online. Pick your country or region below to get the information you need.
  
    
    


> [!NOTE]
> To see if Calling Plans are available in your country or region, see  [Países o regiones que se pueden llamar a los planes y las conferencias de Audio](countries-regions-that-are-supported-for-audio-conferencing-and-calling-plans.md)
  
    
    


To help you pick the type of phone number you need, read  [Diferentes tipos de números de teléfono utilizados en Skype empresarial Online](different-kinds-of-phone-numbers-used-for-calling-plans.md).
  
    
    


To learn more about phone numbers, read  [Obtener números de teléfono de Skype Empresarial para los usuarios](getting-phone-numbers-for-your-users.md).
  
    
    


## Service numbers

For service numbers, if the country or region is listed but there aren't service numbers available in the **Skype for Business admin center**, send us a new phone number request.
  
    
    

- For service numbers in the U.S., please  [download](http://download.microsoft.com/download/6/A/7/6A7CFADC-30FD-4033-BCD6-1E027DAD2336/New Phone Number Request for the U.S. %28Service numbers%29 (v.1) (en.US).pdf) and fill out the form then [send it to us](mailto:ptn@microsoft.com).
    
  
- For service numbers outside of the U.S., please  [download](http://download.microsoft.com/download/6/A/7/6A7CFADC-30FD-4033-BCD6-1E027DAD2336/New Phone Number Request for all countries except the U.S. %28Service numbers%29 (v.1) (en.US).pdf) and fill out the form then [send it to us](mailto:ptneu@microsoft.com).
